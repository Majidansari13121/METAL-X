About Metal X

Metal X is a pioneering leader in scrap metal and plastic recycling, committed to transforming waste into sustainable resources. Our vision is to create a cleaner planet by reducing environmental footprints through innovative recycling technologies. We focus on eco-friendly initiatives and provide top-quality services in scrap collection, processing, and responsible waste management. With decades of industry expertise, Metal X guarantees reliability, efficiency, and commitment to a greener future.
